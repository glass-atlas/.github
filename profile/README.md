# Glass Atlas

Glass Atlas brings clarity, craft and conviction to digital commerce. We help large businesses solve complex challenges across commerce technology and performance marketing with sustainable, measurable growth in mind.

## What we do
Two practices. One outcome: sustainable, measurable growth.

- **Commerce Technology**
  - Platforms built for the long haul.
  - **Technology**: platform selection and build for ecommerce.
  - **Integrations**: connect systems so they talk to each other.
  - **Maintenance & Support**: keep sites healthy and secure.

- **Performance Marketing**
  - Digital marketing that drives the numbers.
  - **CRM**: retain and nurture existing customers.
  - **CRO**: convert more visitors into customers.
  - **Data & Analytics**: make better data-led decisions.

## How we work
We do the right thing—clear thinking, honest conversations, and delivery with care and commercial purpose. We partner closely with client teams, tackling complex problems with engineering rigour and an emphasis on long-term value, accessibility and measurable outcomes.
